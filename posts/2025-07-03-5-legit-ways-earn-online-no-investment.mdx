date: 2025-07-03

No startup cash? Here are five **no‑investment** gigs you can start today:

1. **Freelance Writing** – Platforms: Upwork, Fiverr.  
2. **Transcription & Data Entry** – Try Rev.com or local agencies.  
3. **Online Tutoring** – Teach English or Math on Preply.  
4. **Affiliate Marketing** – Promote Jumia links on social media.  
5. **Micro‑tasks** – Sign up to Clickworker, Amazon MTurk.  

> **Avoid** “pay‑to‑register” sites—real opportunities won’t charge you fees.
