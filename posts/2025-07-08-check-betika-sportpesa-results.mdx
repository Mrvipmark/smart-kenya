---
title: "How to Check Betika or SportPesa Results Instantly"
date: 2025-07-08
---

Quick ways to see if you won:

- **USSD**: Dial `*290#` (Betika) or `*290*8#` (SportPesa).  
- **SMS**: Send “RESULT <betID>” to 29090.  
- **Mobile Apps**: Install Betika or SportPesa app → Results tab.  

> Keep your bet slip ID handy for fast queries.
