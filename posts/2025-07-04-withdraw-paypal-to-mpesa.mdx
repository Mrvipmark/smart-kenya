---
title: "How to Withdraw Money from PayPal to M-Pesa (2025 Guide)"
date: 2025-07-04
---

Here’s a **step‑by‑step** on moving funds from PayPal into your M‑Pesa:

1. Log into your **PayPal** account → **Withdraw Funds**.  
2. Choose **Mobile Money** → enter your **M‑Pesa number** (07XX XXX XXX).  
3. Confirm and wait ~24 hrs.  
4. Fees: ~2.5 % + Ksh 100 withdrawal charge.  
5. Check your M‑Pesa statement for the transaction.

> **Pro Tip:** Ensure your PayPal is fully verified to avoid holds.
